# Launch Checklist — Jacksonville Handyman Business
## Step-by-Step: Get Leads Flowing by Friday

---

## BEFORE YOU START (30 min)

### Get These Ready First
- [ ] **Photos:** 4-8 photos per service (wildlife, roofing, handyman, property maintenance). Before/after shots, you at work, your truck. If you don't have enough yet, use stock photos temporarily — replace with real ones ASAP
- [ ] **Phone number:** Decide on tracking. Options:
  - Simple: Use your real number for now, get tracking later
  - Better: Set up a Google Voice number (free) — forward to your phone
  - Best: CallRail tracking number ($45/mo) — separate number per service type
- [ ] **Business name:** Decide what name(s) to use on ads. Can use multiple (helps with Craigslist variety): e.g., "Jax Wildlife Solutions," "Northeast FL Handyman," "Duval Property Maintenance"
- [ ] **Your rates:** Know what you're charging before calls come in:
  - Wildlife removal: $150–$400 per job
  - Roof repair: $300–$2,500 depending on scope
  - Handyman work: $200–$500 for most jobs, up to $5K for bigger ones
  - Property maintenance: Hourly ($50-75/hr) or per-job quotes

## STEP 1: CRAIGSLIST (1-2 hours)

### Option A: Use a Posting Service (Easiest — Do This If You Want Leads Tomorrow)
1. [ ] Go to **craigslistdominator.com** → Sign up → Get $10 free credit
2. [ ] Fund account with $70–$100 (enough for 7-10 ads)
3. [ ] Submit your 7 ads for Jacksonville:

| Ad # | Service | Category | Title Example |
|------|---------|----------|---------------|
| 1 | Wildlife Removal | Skilled Trade Services | "Wildlife Removal — Raccoons, Squirrels, Rats — Jacksonville FL" |
| 2 | Wildlife Removal | Household Services | "Animal Problems? Trapping & Exclusion — Same Day — Jax Area" |
| 3 | Roof Repair | Skilled Trade Services | "Roof Leak Repair — Licensed & Insured — Jacksonville" |
| 4 | Roof Repair | Household Services | "Storm Damage? Fast Roof Repair — Free Estimates — Jax FL" |
| 5 | Property Maintenance | Skilled Trade Services | "On-Call Property Maintenance — Investors & Landlords — Jax" |
| 6 | Property Maintenance | Household Services | "Full Service Property Maintenance — Jacksonville Area" |
| 7 | Handyman | Skilled Trade Services | "Handyman Services — Drywall, Fixtures, Painting — Jacksonville FL" |

4. [ ] Upload 4-8 photos per ad (different photos for each!)
5. [ ] Include your phone number at top AND bottom of each ad
6. [ ] Submit — they handle the rest. Check dashboard for live confirmations

### Option B: Fiverr Freelancer (Cheapest)
1. [ ] Go to fiverr.com → Search "craigslist posting service"
2. [ ] Find seller with 4.8+ stars and 100+ reviews
3. [ ] Message them: "I need 7 service ads posted in Jacksonville FL — skilled trade services and household services. I have all copy and photos ready. Do you guarantee live ads?"
4. [ ] Send them ad copy (from `automation/craigslist/ad_templates/templates.yaml`) + photos
5. [ ] Pay for confirmed live ads only

### Option C: DIY from the Jacksonville MacBook
1. [ ] Have Maudi leave MacBook on and connected to WiFi
2. [ ] Set up remote access (Chrome Remote Desktop — free and easy)
3. [ ] Create 3 Craigslist accounts (each needs unique email + phone number)
4. [ ] Post ads manually following templates in `automation/craigslist/ad_templates/templates.yaml`
5. [ ] Renew every 48 hours

**CL Budget: $35–$70/day (7 ads × $5–$10 each depending on method)**

## STEP 2: FACEBOOK MARKETPLACE (1 hour)

### Post 7 Product-Style Listings
Facebook doesn't allow services directly. Use these workarounds:

| Ad # | Service | FB Title | Price | Category |
|------|---------|----------|-------|----------|
| 1 | Wildlife | "FREE Wildlife Inspection — Raccoon & Squirrel Problems" | $0 (Free) | Classifieds > Miscellaneous |
| 2 | Wildlife | "Wildlife Removal Gift Certificate — Jax Area" | $50 | Classifieds > Miscellaneous |
| 3 | Roof Repair | "FREE Roof Leak Assessment — Storm Damage Check" | $0 (Free) | Home Improvement Supplies |
| 4 | Roof Repair | "Roof Repair Kit + Free Professional Install" | $99 | Home Improvement Supplies |
| 5 | Property Maint | "Monthly Property Maintenance Package — Jacksonville" | $149 | Classifieds > Miscellaneous |
| 6 | Property Maint | "FREE Property Maintenance Consultation — Landlords" | $0 (Free) | Classifieds > Miscellaneous |
| 7 | Handyman | "Handyman Service Gift Certificate — All Repairs" | $50 | Classifieds > Miscellaneous |

### Rules to Not Get Flagged:
- [ ] Post from the Facebook app on your phone (not desktop — longer survival)
- [ ] Max 1-2 listings per day for the first 2 weeks (new accounts)
- [ ] Use real photos, not stock
- [ ] Don't say "service" in the title — frame it as a product
- [ ] If a listing gets taken down, don't repost the same one. Change everything

### Set Up Auto-Response:
- [ ] **Free option:** Turn on Facebook Business Suite auto-replies (Settings → Messaging → Instant Reply)
- [ ] **Better option:** Check messages manually every 2-3 hours and respond immediately
- [ ] **Best option:** FB Auto Reply AI Chrome extension (~$30/mo) — auto-responds with AI

**FB Budget: $0 (free listings) or $5-15/day for paid FB ads later**

## STEP 4: AI PHONE SYSTEM (1 hour)

### Quick Setup (Do This First)
1. [ ] **Google Voice number** — free, forwards to Maudi's phone
2. [ ] Set up voicemail greeting: "Thanks for calling [Business Name]. We're either on a job or on another call. Leave your name, number, and what you need help with, and we'll call you back within 30 minutes."
3. [ ] Both Maudi and you get notifications when calls come in

### Better Setup (Do This Week)
1. [ ] **OpenPhone** ($15/mo) or **Grasshopper** ($14/mo) — business phone line with auto-attendant
2. [ ] Set up call forwarding: Rings Maudi first → if no answer, rolls to AI voicemail → texts you both
3. [ ] Auto-text reply to missed calls: "Hey! Thanks for calling [Business Name]. We're on another call — what do you need help with? We'll get back to you in the next 30 minutes."

### Best Setup (Next Week)
1. [ ] Deploy AI receptionist (Bland.ai or Vapi.ai — $0.07-0.12/min)
2. [ ] AI answers: "Hi, you've reached [Business Name], how can I help you today?"
3. [ ] AI captures: name, address, what they need, when they're available
4. [ ] AI books into calendar or texts you the lead details
5. [ ] Estimated cost: $50-100/month based on call volume

2. [ ] Pull list of homes sold in last 90 days
3. [ ] Direct mail or cold call: "Congrats on the new home! I'm a local handyman — if you need anything installed, fixed, or maintained, I'm your guy."
