## Lead Gen Channels Researched

### Tier 1 — Primary (Free / Near-Free, High ROI)
| Channel | Cost | Best For |
|---------|------|----------|
| Google Business Profile | Free | Foundation — shows in local search/maps |
| Craigslist | $7-10/post | High-intent, price-conscious leads |
| Nextdoor | Free page | 77% homeowners, neighborhood trust |
| Facebook Marketplace | Free | Volume, younger demographics |

### Tier 2 — Supplemental (Free Listings)
| Channel | Cost | Best For |
|---------|------|----------|
| Home Depot Pro Referral | Points-based (free) | Passive leads from HD shoppers |
| Yelp (free listing) | Free | Review-driven trust |
| Houzz (free profile) | Free | Portfolio showcase for renovations |
| BuildZoom | 2.5% only if hired | Zero upfront risk |

### Tier 3 — Paid (Use Selectively)
| Channel | Cost | Best For |
|---------|------|----------|
| Google Local Services Ads | Pay per lead | Highest quality, Google Guaranteed |
| Thumbtack | $15-100+/lead | Declining quality — use carefully |
| Angi/HomeAdvisor | $15-80+/lead | Shared leads — often not worth it |
