## Lead Gen Channels Covered

| Channel | Status | Detail Level |
|---------|--------|-------------|
| **Craigslist** | ✅ Deep dive | Posting rules, ad templates, multi-city strategy, automation |
| **Home Depot Pro Referral** | ✅ Researched | Points-based, zero upfront cost |
| **Porch** | ✅ Researched | Lead quality, pricing, ROI analysis |
| **Facebook Marketplace** | ✅ Researched | Limitations for services, workarounds |
| **Google Local Services Ads** | ✅ Researched | #1 recommended channel |
| **Nextdoor** | ✅ Researched | 77% homeowner audience, high close rates |
| **Thumbtack** | ✅ Researched | Declining quality, rising costs |
| **Angi/HomeAdvisor** | ✅ Researched | Shared leads, aggressive contracts |
| **Yelp** | ✅ Researched | Free listing value |
| **BuildZoom** | ✅ Researched | 2.5% commission only if hired |
| **Houzz** | ✅ Researched | Free portfolio, paid lead gen not worth it |
| **TaskRabbit** | ✅ Researched | Commission-based, small jobs |
| **OfferUp** | ✅ Researched | Free, low quality |
| **Google Business Profile** | ✅ Researched | Foundation channel, free |

## Offers Overview

1. **Cold Email Machine** — Automated outreach to property managers, landlords, commercial accounts
2. **AI SDR** — AI sales development rep for qualifying and booking leads
3. **Lean Operations Hub** — Scheduling, dispatch, invoicing, CRM
4. **One-Call Content Machine** — Turn one phone call into a month of marketing content
5. **Ops Home Base** — Complete operations management system

## Quick Start

1. Read `research/MARKETPLACE-LEAD-GEN-RESEARCH-2025-2026.md` for the full platform breakdown
2. Pick your top 3-5 lead gen channels from the research
3. Choose your offer tier from `offers/`
4. Build your social selling system from `lead-gen/Social_SDR_Plan.md`
5. Use `swipe-file/` for copy inspiration on ads, emails, and landing pages
