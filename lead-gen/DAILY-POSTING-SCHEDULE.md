# Daily Posting & Lead Gen Schedule

## Morning Routine (30-45 min, before first job)

### 7:00 AM — Lead Check & Response
- [ ] Check all channels for overnight leads (Craigslist email, Marketplace messages, Nextdoor DMs, Google messages)
- [ ] Respond to EVERY lead within 5 minutes of seeing it
- [ ] Log new leads in tracking spreadsheet

### 7:15 AM — Nextdoor Scan
- [ ] Open Nextdoor → search "handyman", "repair", "fix", "need help"
- [ ] Check "Recommendations" section for new requests
- [ ] Respond personally to any relevant requests
- [ ] Time spent: 10 minutes max

### 7:25 AM — Craigslist Post/Renew
- [ ] If 48+ hours since last post → Post new ad (rotate template)
- [ ] If existing ads are 48+ hours old → Hit "Renew" button
- [ ] Check Craigslist "gigs > labor" for subcontractor opportunities
- [ ] Time spent: 10 minutes max

### 7:35 AM — Facebook Marketplace
- [ ] Post 1 new listing (fresh photo + description)
- [ ] Check Marketplace messages for inquiries
- [ ] Quick scan of 2-3 local Facebook groups for service requests
- [ ] Time spent: 10 minutes max

---

## Midday Check (5-10 min, during lunch)

### 12:00 PM
- [ ] Check all channels for new leads
- [ ] Respond to any unanswered messages
- [ ] Follow up on morning leads that haven't replied

---

## End of Day (15-20 min, after last job)

### 5:00 PM — Job Completion
- [ ] Take before/after photos of today's jobs
- [ ] Send review request text to today's customers
- [ ] Update tracking spreadsheet with completed jobs

### 5:15 PM — Next Day Prep
- [ ] Confirm tomorrow's appointments (text each customer)
- [ ] Prepare tomorrow's Craigslist ad (write if new, queue renewal if not)
- [ ] Review lead pipeline — anyone need a follow-up tomorrow?

## Monthly Calendar

### Week 1
- [ ] Update Google Business Profile (new photos, seasonal post)
- [ ] Post 1 before/after showcase on Nextdoor
- [ ] Cold email 5 new property managers
- [ ] Review last month's lead metrics

### Week 2
- [ ] Update Yelp profile (new photos)
- [ ] Attend 1 local networking event or open house
- [ ] Post seasonal offer on all platforms
- [ ] Check Home Depot Pro Referral for any leads

### Week 3
- [ ] Post helpful content in Facebook groups (2-3 groups)
- [ ] Cold email 5 new property managers
- [ ] Test a new Craigslist ad variation
- [ ] Review which ads/platforms are performing best

### Week 4
- [ ] Monthly metrics review (see CHANNEL-STRATEGY.md)
- [ ] Kill underperforming ads, create new variations
- [ ] Plan next month's seasonal messaging
- [ ] Collect and organize new before/after photos
