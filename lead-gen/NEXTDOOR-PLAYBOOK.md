# Nextdoor Playbook — Handyman Services

## Why Nextdoor is a Top Channel

- 77% of users are homeowners (your exact target)
- Users are 161% more likely to be interested in remodeling/construction
- 67% share service provider recommendations
- 94% trust rate for recommendations
- Reported 44% close rates on Nextdoor leads
- Hyper-local by design — organized by verified neighborhoods

---

## Setup

### Free Business Page
1. Go to business.nextdoor.com
2. Claim your business (or create new)
3. Complete EVERY field — name, address, phone, hours, service area
4. Add 10+ photos (before/after work, truck, team)
5. Select ALL relevant service categories
6. Write a description that matches how neighbors talk (not corporate)

### Neighborhood Coverage
- Nextdoor lets you set a service radius
- Start with your immediate neighborhoods (5-10 mile radius)
- Expand as you get recommendations in neighboring areas

---

## Daily Monitoring Protocol

### What to Monitor (Check 2-3x Daily)
1. **"Recommendations" requests** — People posting "Looking for a good handyman" or "Anyone know someone who can fix X?"
2. **"For Sale & Free" section** — Sometimes people post service requests here
3. **Direct messages** — Leads from your business page

### Response Template for Recommendation Requests
```
Hi [Name]! I'd be happy to help with [their specific need].
I'm a local handyman based right here in [neighborhood] —
licensed, insured, and I've done a lot of work in this area.

I can usually get out there same-day or next-day for a free estimate.
Feel free to send me a DM or call/text me at [number].

Happy to send some before/after photos of similar work I've done nearby!
```

### Key Rules
- **Be helpful first, sell second** — answer questions even if you don't get the job
- **Never hard-sell** — Nextdoor users flag overly promotional posts
- **Mention your neighborhood** — "I'm based in [area]" builds trust
- **Respond FAST** — First responder gets 50%+ of these jobs

## Proactive Posting Strategy

### Weekly Post Types (Rotate)

**Type 1: Helpful Tip**
```
🔧 Quick tip for homeowners in [Neighborhood]:

With [season] coming, now is a great time to [seasonal tip —
check gutters, inspect deck, test smoke detectors, etc.].

Here's how to check: [brief how-to]

If you find any issues or just want a professional to take a
look, I'm happy to swing by. I'm a local handyman right here
in [neighborhood] — just shoot me a message.

Have a great week, neighbors!
```

**Type 2: Before/After Project Showcase**
```
Just wrapped up a [project type] for a neighbor in [nearby area]!

[Brief description of the work and the problem it solved]

If you've got something similar on your to-do list, I'd be happy
to come take a look. Free estimates, flat-rate pricing.

📞 [Number] or DM me here.
```

**Type 3: Seasonal Offer**
```
Hey neighbors — spring is here and I'm booking up fast for:
• Pressure washing
• Deck staining
• Gutter cleaning
• Exterior painting

If you've been putting it off, now's the time.
I'm offering 10% off for [Neighborhood] residents this month.

Call/text [Number] or DM me to get on the schedule.
```

### Posting Rules
- DO NOT post more than 1x per week (you'll get flagged)
- Always provide value, not just promotion
- Vary your content — tips, projects, offers, community engagement
- Reply to every comment on your posts
- Thank people who recommend you publicly

## Review / Recommendation Collection

### After Every Nextdoor Job
```
Text to customer:

"Hi [Name], thanks again for having us out today!
If you're happy with the work, it would mean the world if you
could leave a quick recommendation on Nextdoor. It really helps
neighbors find reliable help. Here's the link: [your business page URL]

Thanks so much! Let me know if anything comes up in the future."
```

### Why Recommendations Are Gold
- Nextdoor recommendations show up when neighbors search for services
- Each recommendation is visible to hundreds of neighbors
- Social proof in a trusted community context = highest conversion

## Automation Opportunities

### What to Automate (in the Lead Automation Repo)
1. **Monitor for new recommendation requests** — Scrape/poll for "looking for handyman" type posts
2. **Instant alert** — Push notification when a new relevant request appears
3. **Auto-draft response** — Pre-fill a personalized response template
4. **Track response times** — Log when requests appear vs when you respond

### What NOT to Automate
- Don't auto-post — Nextdoor will flag/ban automated posting
- Don't auto-respond — Responses should feel personal and human
- Don't mass-message — This violates Nextdoor's terms

### Semi-Automation Approach
1. Bot monitors for new requests every 5-15 minutes
2. Sends you a push notification with the request details
3. You open the app and respond personally within 2-3 minutes
4. Bot logs the interaction for tracking
