# Craigslist Playbook — Handyman Services

## Quick Reference

- **Category:** Skilled trade services (sks)
- **Cost:** $7-10 per post
- **Renewal:** Every 48 hours (use renew button, don't repost)
- **Rule:** 1 post per category per metro per 48 hours
- **Phone verification:** Required (real number, no VOIP)

### Account Setup Per Metro
- 1 Craigslist account per metro area
- 1 unique phone number per account (for verification)
- 1 unique tracking phone number per ad (for lead attribution)
- Geo-targeted IP for each metro (residential proxy or actual location)

---

## Ghosting Prevention Checklist

Before every post, verify:
- [ ] Title is unique (not copy-pasted from last post)
- [ ] Description is unique (rewritten, not just reordered)
- [ ] Photos are unique to this post (not reused from another ad)
- [ ] At least 48 hours since last post in this category/metro
- [ ] IP address matches the target metro
- [ ] No ALL CAPS in title
- [ ] No excessive symbols or emojis
- [ ] 1-2 links maximum (to your website)
- [ ] Contact phone number is included
- [ ] Post is in the correct category (skilled trade services)

---

## Tracking Setup

### For Each Ad
1. Get a unique tracking phone number (Google Voice, CallRail, or Twilio)
2. Use a unique URL with UTM parameters: `yoursite.com/?utm_source=craigslist&utm_medium=sks&utm_campaign=city-template1`
3. Log in spreadsheet: Date | City | Template # | Tracking Number | Calls | Emails | Jobs Booked | Revenue

### Monthly Review
- Which city generates the most leads?
- Which template converts best?
- What's the cost per lead per city?
- What's the cost per booked job?
- Kill underperformers, double down on winners
