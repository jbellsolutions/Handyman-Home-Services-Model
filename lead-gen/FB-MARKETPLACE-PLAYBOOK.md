# Facebook Marketplace & Groups Playbook — Handyman Services

## Facebook Marketplace

### The Reality
- Facebook Marketplace does NOT officially support service listings
- It's designed for physical product sales
- BUT contractors work around this by posting as "items" with service descriptions
- Risk: Posts can get removed, account can get restricted if flagged

### How Contractors Use It
1. Post a "listing" with a service photo (before/after, truck, team)
2. Title: "Handyman Services — [City] — Same Day Available"
3. Price: Set to $0 or $1 (or "Free" to get clicks) or list your starting rate
4. Description: Full service description, contact info, service area
5. Category: Choose "Home & Garden" or "Home Services" if available
6. Location: Set to your primary service area

### Posting Strategy
- Post 1-2 new listings daily (different photos/descriptions each time)
- Delete old listings after 7 days and repost fresh
- Use different angles/photos for each post
- Include your phone number in the first line of every description
- Cover photo should be your best before/after shot

## Facebook Groups (Higher Value Than Marketplace)

### Groups to Join (In Every Metro You Serve)
1. "[City] Community" groups
2. "[City] Buy/Sell/Trade" groups
3. "[City] Homeowners" groups
4. "[Neighborhood] Neighbors" groups
5. "[City] Moms" groups (huge for handyman referrals)
6. "[City] Real Estate Investors" groups
7. "Landlords of [City]" groups
8. "[City] Property Managers" groups
9. "[City] Recommendations" groups

### Group Engagement Strategy

**DON'T:** Spam your services, post ads, hard-sell
**DO:** Be helpful, answer questions, build reputation

#### Weekly Group Routine (30 min/week)
1. **Monday:** Search all groups for "handyman", "repair", "fix" — respond to 3-5 posts
2. **Wednesday:** Post a helpful tip or before/after in 1-2 groups (if group rules allow)
3. **Friday:** Reply to any pending threads, thank people who tagged you

#### Response Template (When Someone Asks for Recommendations)
```
Hey [Name]! I can help with that. I'm a local handyman
in [area] — licensed and insured. I've done a lot of
[their specific need] around here.

Happy to come take a look and give you a free estimate.
Shoot me a DM or text me at [Number]!

[Before/after photo if relevant]
```

#### Helpful Post Template (Not Selling, Just Helping)
```
Quick PSA for [City] homeowners 🏠

If you've noticed [seasonal issue — drafty windows, gutter overflow,
deck boards warping], here's a quick fix you can try:

[2-3 sentence DIY tip]

If it's bigger than a DIY project, feel free to reach out.
I'm a local handyman and happy to help.

Stay safe out there, neighbors!
```

## Facebook Business Page

### Setup (One-Time)
1. Create a Facebook Business Page (not personal profile)
2. Category: "Handyman" or "Home Improvement"
3. Complete all info: address, phone, hours, service area, website
4. Add 20+ photos (before/after, team, truck, jobs)
5. Enable Messenger for lead capture
6. Set up automated greeting in Messenger

### Messenger Auto-Greeting
```
Hi! 👋 Thanks for reaching out to [Business Name].

We're a local handyman team serving [City] and surrounding areas.

To get a quick quote, just tell us:
1. What needs to be done?
2. Your general location?
3. When do you need it done?

We'll get back to you within 15 minutes during business hours!
```

### Weekly Page Activity
- Post 1-2 times per week (project photos, tips, reviews)
- Share Google reviews to your page
- Respond to every comment and message
- Run occasional boosted posts ($5-20) targeting homeowners in your zip codes

## Automation Opportunities

### What to Automate (in the Lead Automation Repo)
1. **Facebook Group monitoring** — Scrape/poll target groups for service request keywords
2. **Marketplace posting** — Auto-post listings with rotation (high ban risk — use carefully)
3. **Messenger auto-reply** — Facebook's built-in tools or ManyChat for initial response
4. **Lead capture** — Forward Marketplace inquiries and Messenger leads to CRM

### What NOT to Automate
- Group commenting/responding (feels robotic, gets you banned from groups)
- Mass posting the same content to multiple groups simultaneously
- Fake engagement (likes, comments) on your own posts

### Ban Prevention
- Never post identical content across groups
- Space out group posts by 30+ minutes
- Use aged Facebook accounts (not new ones)
- Don't join more than 5 new groups per day
- If restricted, stop all activity for 48-72 hours
