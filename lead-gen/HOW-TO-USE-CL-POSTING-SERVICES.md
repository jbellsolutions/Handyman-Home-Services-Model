# How to Use Craigslist Posting Services
## Quick-Start Guide for Jacksonville Handyman Business

---

## The Short Version

You pay a service to post your Craigslist ads for you. They handle accounts, phone verification, proxies, and ghosting prevention. You provide the ad copy and photos.

---

## Step 1: Pick Your Service

### Best Options (ranked):

**Option A: Craigslist Dominator** — Best if you want hands-off
- Website: craigslistdominator.com
- Cost: $5/ad (their fee) + $5/ad (CL fee) = **$10 per live ad**
- $10 free posting credit on signup
- Also posts to Facebook Marketplace
- 100% live ad guarantee (you don't pay if the ad gets ghosted)
- How to start: Sign up → fund account → submit your ads → they post

**Option B: CraigslistBiz** — Best for structured monthly plans
- Website: craigslistbiz.com
- Monthly packages starting ~$450/month + CL fees
- Includes ad writing and optimization
- How to start: Pick a package → send them your business info → they handle everything

**Option C: Fiverr Freelancer** — Best for testing cheaply
- Search "craigslist posting service" on Fiverr
- Cost: $0.50-$2 per live ad (their fee) + $5/ad (CL fee)
- How to start: Find a seller with 100+ reviews and 4.8+ stars → send them your ad templates → they post
- Risk: less reliable, some ads may get ghosted and they'll still count them

**Option D: Filipino VA** — Best value at scale
- Hire from OnlineJobs.ph ($3-6/hour)
- Cost: ~$400-600/month for a part-time VA + CL fees
- You train them with SOPs (we have templates ready)
- How to start: Post a job → hire → train them using the posting SOPs below

## Step 4: If Using Craigslist Dominator (Walkthrough)

1. Go to **craigslistdominator.com**
2. Click **Sign Up** → create account
3. **Fund your account** — start with $100 (gets you ~10 ads including CL fees)
4. Go to **Post an Ad** → select:
   - City: **Jacksonville, FL**
   - Category: **Services > Skilled Trade Services** (or Household Services)
5. Paste your ad title and body from our templates
6. Upload your photos (they'll tell you format/size requirements)
7. Enter your contact info
8. Submit — they handle posting, phone verification, and anti-ghosting
9. Check your dashboard for **live confirmation** — they guarantee the ad is visible
10. Repeat for each of your 7 daily ads

---

## Step 5: If Using a Fiverr Freelancer (Walkthrough)

1. Go to **fiverr.com**
2. Search: **"craigslist posting service"**
3. Filter by: 4.8+ stars, 100+ reviews, USA/English-speaking
4. Look for sellers who specifically mention:
   - "Services section" posting
   - "Live ad guarantee"
   - "Include CL posting fees" (some do, some don't — ask)
5. Message them BEFORE ordering:
   > "I need 7 service ads posted daily in Jacksonville FL — skilled trade services and household services categories. I'll provide all ad copy and photos. Do you guarantee live ads? Do your prices include CL's $5 posting fee or is that separate?"
6. Send them:
   - Your 7 ad templates (from `templates.yaml`)
   - Your photos (organized by service)
   - Your contact info
   - Target categories
7. They post → send you screenshots of live ads
8. Pay only for confirmed live ads

## Step 6: If Posting Yourself (DIY from the Jax MacBook)

If your person in Jacksonville can do it:

1. **Create 3 CL accounts** (each needs its own email + phone number)
2. **Age the accounts** — browse CL, post a few free "for sale" items first
3. **Post from the MacBook** — the Jacksonville IP is the most authentic
4. Follow the 48-hour rule: 1 post per category per city per 48 hours per account
5. Use our templates: `automation/craigslist/ad_templates/templates.yaml`
6. **Rotate content** — never post the same title/body/photos twice in a row
7. **Renew** existing posts after 48 hours instead of creating duplicates

### Account Distribution:
- Account 1: Wildlife Removal (2 posts) + Handyman (1 post) = 3 posts
- Account 2: Roof Repair (2 posts) + 1 extra = 3 posts
- Account 3: Property Maintenance (2 posts) = 2 posts (leaves room for growth)

### Time required: ~30-45 minutes per day

## Important Rules to Avoid Getting Banned

1. **48-hour rule is sacred** — 1 post per category per city per 48 hours per account
2. **Every ad must be unique** — different title, body, and photos each time
3. **No ALL-CAPS titles** — looks spammy, gets flagged
4. **Include real contact info** — ads without phone numbers get flagged by users
5. **Don't overlink** — 1 URL max per ad (your website or booking link)
6. **Post from a consistent IP** — Jax MacBook or ISP proxy, not random VPNs
7. **If ghosted, stop for 48 hours** — then come back with completely fresh content
