## Channel-by-Channel Breakdown

### 1. Google Business Profile (FOUNDATION)
**Priority: #1 — Set up FIRST, maintain ALWAYS**
- Free listing that shows in Google Maps and local search
- Drives 40-60% of leads for most handymen
- Must have: 20+ reviews at 4.8+ stars, photos of every job, complete service list
- Post weekly updates (Google Posts) with seasonal offers
- Respond to every review (positive and negative)

### 2. Craigslist (HIGH VOLUME)
**Priority: #2 — Daily posting engine**
- $7-10/post, renew every 48 hours
- Post under "skilled trade services"
- Rotate 5-10 unique ad variations
- Multi-city posting on a staggered schedule
- Leads are price-sensitive — lead with free estimates and entry-point offers
- **Full playbook:** See CRAIGSLIST-PLAYBOOK.md

### 3. Nextdoor (HIGH TRUST)
**Priority: #3 — Monitor and respond daily**
- 77% of users are homeowners
- Free business page + respond to "recommendations" requests
- SCRAPE/MONITOR: Check daily for "looking for a handyman" posts
- Respond within minutes — first responder wins
- Build reputation through helpful comments, not just ads
- **Full playbook:** See NEXTDOOR-PLAYBOOK.md

### 4. Facebook Marketplace + Groups (VOLUME + COMMUNITY)
**Priority: #4 — Daily posting + weekly community engagement**
- Marketplace: Post service listings (not officially supported for services, but works in practice)
- Groups: Join every local homeowner, neighborhood, and community group
- Provide helpful answers → build reputation → get DMs
- Never hard-sell in groups — be the helpful expert
- **Full playbook:** See FB-MARKETPLACE-PLAYBOOK.md

### 5. Google Local Services Ads (PAID — BEST ROI)
**Priority: #5 — Turn on when ready for paid leads**
- Pay per lead (not per click), $15-50 per lead depending on service
- "Google Guaranteed" badge builds massive trust
- ~31% conversion rate from lead to booked job
- Requires background check and insurance verification
- Start at $500/month budget, scale based on ROI

### 6. OfferUp (SUPPLEMENTAL)
- Free to post, easy mobile app
- Lower quality leads but zero cost
- Good for simple tasks: furniture assembly, moving help, junk removal
- Post weekly, renew regularly

### 7. Home Depot Pro Referral (PASSIVE)
- Points-based, effectively free
- Set up profile and forget — leads trickle in
- Better if you already buy materials at Home Depot

### 8. BuildZoom (ZERO RISK)
- 2.5% commission only if customer hires you
- No upfront cost, no monthly fee
- Good for larger renovation projects

### 9. Yelp (FREE LISTING ONLY)
- Free listing is valuable for SEO and reviews
- DO NOT pay for Yelp ads — terrible ROI for handyman services
- Focus on collecting reviews from happy customers

### 10. Property Manager Direct Outreach (HIGHEST LTV)
- Cold email property management companies
- Offer a free first visit or discounted first month
- Target: PMs with 10+ units who need a reliable go-to handyman
- This is where retainer revenue comes from

## Lead Response Protocol

### Speed Wins
```
Goal: Respond to EVERY lead in under 5 minutes

Craigslist email → Auto-forward to phone → Call back within 5 min
Nextdoor request → Push notification → Respond in-app within 5 min
Facebook message → Messenger notification → Reply within 5 min
Google call/message → Phone rings → Answer live or call back within 5 min
```

### First Response Script
```
"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] with [Business]. Thanks for reaching out!
I can definitely help with [their project]. I have availability
[today/tomorrow/this week]. Would [specific time] work for a
free estimate? I'll come take a look and give you a flat-rate price
on the spot."
```

### Follow-Up Cadence
- Lead comes in → Respond in 5 min
- No reply → Follow up 2 hours later
- No reply → Follow up next morning
- No reply → Follow up 48 hours later (final)
- If they quoted but didn't book → Follow up in 3 days
- If they booked → Confirm day before, text morning-of
